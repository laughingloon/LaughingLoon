1921 - 2013
Mom loved the flowers at the Maine Botanic Gardens and we visited in the spring 2013 to see the tulips!
Dorothy (Fago) Redemske, 92, passed away peacefully on Dec. 29 in Chatham Mass. Dorothy lived with her daughter Sandra Redemske and her husband Robert Macks in Jefferson, Maine for over six years.

Born in Chicago, Illinois, she attended local schools and in 1943 married Robert Redemske. A loving mother, she stayed at home to raise her family, using her creative talents to make a wonderful home. In 2007, Dorothy moved to Maine, She loved music and really enjoyed attending local musical events. Also, a member of the Coastal Maine Botanical Gardens, she enjoyed visiting the gardens in all seasons. Dorothy had a gift of caring for others by keeping in touch with friends and relatives by sending many cards and letters.

Dorothy is survived by five children; Richard, Joyce Young, Gale (James) Morrow, Sandra (Robert Macks) Redemske and John (Susan DeCloedt) Redemske; devoted grandmother of Barnaby (Leigh) Young, Gretchen (David) Caramiciu, Ben Young, Julia Redemske, Erik Redemske (deceased), Kirsten (Julie  Elkins)  Morrow, Katherine Morrow, Peter Morrow; great-grandmother of six, and aunt of many nieces and nephews.
She was predeceased by husband Robert, sisters Mary Fago, Wilma (Fago) Johnson, brothers Louis Fago, Frank Fago, and Charles Fago. 

A celebration of her life will be held at a later date.

Memorial donations may be made to the Coastal Maine Botanical Gardens,
