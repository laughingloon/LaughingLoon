Cancellation Fees on Boat Construction

Due to the nature of custom work cancellation fees on boat deposits are as follows.

30 days 5%

60 days 10%

90 days 20%

Commencement of work - deposit is non refundable

Cancellation Fees on Classes

If you cancel 45 days prior to class date your deposit will be refunded minus a $30 fee.

If you cancel with less than 45 days notice your fees will not be refunded but can be credited toward a future class.

We reserve the right to cancel classes for emergency or other reasons in which case deposits will be refunded.
