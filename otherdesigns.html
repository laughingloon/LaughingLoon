Other designs, special modifications and prototypes
Below this North Star sea kayak was modified for a customer with a disability, who wished for a more stable calm water crusing boat. The cockpit coaming was enlarged and raised for easier access and outriggers installed for added stability.

The outriggers are removable for transport by turning the large knobs on top of the outrigger support strut which unscrews the outrigger structure from the kayak deck.
Below is an 18' 6" x 36" pulling boat I built for a customer. The boat weighs 70 pounds.

It has two fixed seat postions and air tight compartments fore and aft with hatch covers held with rare earth magnets.

The customer will outfit this boat with a central sliding seat rigger and tandem rowing positions at the fixed seats.
