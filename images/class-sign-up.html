Tools and Required Safety Equipment

Participants can bring their own tools. A list of appropriate tools are given in the Laughing Loon instruction book provided upon registration. An apron, as described in the Laughing Loon instruction book, is highly recommended.

Tools will be provided at the shop for the use of those without tools of their own.

Participants are required to come to class equipped with safety eye glasses, a box of rubber gloves, ear protectors, and a respirator, with dust and organic vapor cartridges. Details on safety equipment are given in the Laughing Loon instruction book provided upon registration.

Special Consideration

Unfortunately, I have developed an acute allergy to common chemical fragrances used in major brand laundry products like laundry detergents and fabric softeners. Personal care products like soaps, shampoos and deodorants are also a problem for me.

I ask that you work with me on this issue. Besides the fact that it will be difficult to impossible for me to conduct classes, I'm concerned that this problem will escalate and cause me to become sensitive to the materials I use to build boats.

Many people understand the severe peanut allergies that are now common among adults and children.

I hope you can understand and be sensitive to my need to ask for your help in this case.

If you do not consciously choose to buy fragrance free products, you are almost certain to have these fragrance chemicals on your clothes and person. It is extremely difficult to detect fragrances or odors we are in common contact with. I have people come into my shop and tell me how wonderful the cedar smell is. I cannot detect the smell of cedar, I've been around it so long. I have friends who claim they use only fragrance free products however, I still smell these fragrances on them and it makes me sick.

If you use major brands of laundry and personal products every day you are most likely to have a great amount of these chemical fragrances in your cloths and on your body. These chemical fragrances are designed to be very tenacious and long lasting.

Here are a number of options;

If you truly believe you do not have fragrance from these sources, you can come to class with a set of clothing that has been laundered several times in hot water only, and dried on a clothesline outdoors. Please do not use anything but fragrance free hand soap, shampoo and deodorant for several weeks prior to class. If you will do this the changes of my having problems will be greatly reduced.

New clothes are not laundered and do not usually contain these chemicals. If you will buy a tee shirt, sweat shirt and sweat pants of 100% cotton, part of the problem will be solved. Again, please do not use anything but fragrance free hand soap, shampoo and deodorant for several weeks prior to class. If you will do this the chances of my having problems will be greatly reduced.

I can provide tee shirts, sweat pants and sweat shirts to people who still have fragrance that makes me sick.

I will talk with each participant beforehand to work out these issues.
