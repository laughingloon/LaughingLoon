Connie and Roger Ryan
About 12 years ago, Connie asked me to come for Swedish pancakes which she had never made before. When I arrived she was cooking the bacon and something happened and the bacon was a little overcooked...but hey, no big deal. She wanted to set the table nicely even though it was just us so I did that while she worked in the kitchen. All kinds of mishaps occurred, the lingon berry preserves had mold, the toast or muffins got burnt and the pancakes turned into little broken bits. She put everything on a tray and brought it to the table and announced "Martha Stewart, I'm not!"

I could go on but I'm sure everyone has plenty of stories. Connie was one of a kind, for sure. Full of life, with a big heart.

Debbie Rubin
