Sculpture by
Rob Macks
BFA., MA., MFA.
Light colored figures are made of plaster, dark colored ones are cast bronze. All are direct modeled not body casts.

Rob has shown in galleries around the United States and has won awards and one national show.

From 1975 to 1985 Rob taught sculpture and drawing at Wayne State University, Detroit, Mich., Amherst College, Amherst, Mass. and at Leverett Arts and Crafts Center, Leverett, Mass.
